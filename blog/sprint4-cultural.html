Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Benefits of Understanding

An understanding of the principles of neuroplasticity befefits people be showing them that with practice, neural networks grow new connections, strengthen existing ones, and build insulation that speeds transmission of impulses. These neuroscientific discoveries have shown us that we can increase our neural growth by the actions we take, such as using good strategies, asking questions, practicing, and following good nutrition and sleep habits. It gives people the confidence of knowing that through positive attitude and habbits they can recover and improve.

Engaging with the principles of Neuroplasticity

I would like to structure my sleep better and set a time to stop working in the evenings, I get alot done in the mornings and would like to be well rested for that time. Yoga is something I have been engaging with more, I have a bad back anyhow, so it makes sense to stretch and take some time before and after working.

Increasing Neuroplasticity

Key ways to increase Neuroplasticity include:

  • Healthy sleeping habbits.
  • Physical exercise.
  • Continued learning.
  • Reading novels.
  • Reduce stress (e.g. Meditiation.)

Growth Mindset

Growth mindset is a term used by Carol S. Dweck in regards to psycological attitudes towards intelligence and ability. As opposed to a kind of Fixed mindset, which traditionally favors traditional IQ testing as a measure for future ability, Growth mindset is the understanding that through effort and positive practices and habits our brains can be trained and our abilities and learning can improve.

I was suprised to see how similar alot of the materials and excersises around neroplasticity were to practices and hibits to manage depression.
